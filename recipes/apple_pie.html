Apple Pie

Description:

Indulge in the comforting warmth of a homemade classic apple pie, a timeless dessert that brings joy to any gathering. This recipe features a flaky, buttery crust encasing a delightful filling of sweet and tart apples, perfectly spiced with cinnamon and a hint of lemon. Each slice reveals tender apple slices that melt in your mouth, making it a beloved favorite for all ages. Baked to golden perfection, this apple pie is not just a dessert; it's a celebration of home and tradition. Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream for the ultimate treat. Whether enjoyed during holidays or as an everyday indulgence, this classic apple pie is sure to evoke cherished memories and satisfy your sweet cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pie crusts (store-bought or homemade)
  • 6-8 cups apples (Granny Smith or a mix), peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
