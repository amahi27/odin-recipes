Shrimp Carbonara

Description:

Elevate your pasta night with this luscious Shrimp Carbonara, a delightful twist on the classic Italian dish. Succulent shrimp are sautéed to perfection and tossed with al dente spaghetti, creating a satisfying blend of flavors and textures. The creamy sauce, made from eggs and Parmesan cheese, clings to each strand of pasta, delivering a rich and velvety finish. With the savory crunch of pancetta and a hint of garlic, this dish offers a perfect balance of indulgence and freshness. It's quick to prepare, making it an ideal choice for weeknight dinners or special occasions. Serve it hot, garnished with fresh parsley and an extra sprinkle of Parmesan, for a stunning presentation that will impress your family and friends. Whether paired with a crisp salad or enjoyed on its own, this Shrimp Carbonara promises a delightful dining experience that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz of mushrooms (shiitake, cremini, or button), sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Pasta:
    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the rest and set aside.
  2. Sauté the Mushrooms:
    In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 1 tablespoon of butter and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden brown and tender, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the mushrooms from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Cook the Shrimp:
    In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of butter. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the shrimp, season with salt and black pepper, and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Remove from heat.
  4. Prepare the Sauce:
    In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese until well combined. Set aside.
  5. Combine Ingredients:
    Return the drained pasta to the skillet with the shrimp and garlic. Add the sautéed mushrooms and toss to combine. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, stirring rapidly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time until you reach your desired consistency.
  6. Serve:
    Mix well to ensure everything is evenly coated. Adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
